Welcome! I am a second-year Computer Science PhD student at Northeastern University. I am advised by Prof. Tong Xiao and affiliated with NEUNLP Lab. I received my B.Eng in 2017 from Northeastern University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology, and my master in 2020 from Northeastern University, majoring in Computer Software and Theory.

My research interest lies at trustworthy and human-centered AI. I guide NLP models to avoid undesirable behavior [ACL19] [ICLR22]. I also stabilize the dynamics of a deep network according to its continuous formulations [ICML20] to obtain robust and controllable models. I am always fascinated to stand at the intersection of varied perspectives with a unified aim: propose novel deep learning techniques to assist human intelligence and make a positive social impact.
